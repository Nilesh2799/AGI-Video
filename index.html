About AGI Glaspac

Technical knowledge.
Half a century of craft.

We manufacture glass containers under the brand AGI Glaspac to meet the stringent quality standards for packaging needs of food, pharmaceuticals, soft drinks, spirits, beer, wine and other industries.

Our product range varies from a small 5ml Pharma bottle to a 4,000ml chemical and food jar. With the addition of colouring forehearth technology at Bhongir, we can provide glass containers in various colours.

Largest NNPB Manufacturer in India

Narrow Neck Press & Blow technology for superior strength and lightweight packaging.

Stringent Quality Standards

ISO 9000 certified since 1996. Every bottle undergoes rigorous quality checks before dispatch.

Global Exports

Exporting since 2011 to global markets, trusted by international beverage and pharma brands.

Sustainability

Delivering innovation with purpose

Glass is inherently sustainable — 100% recyclable, infinitely. AGI Glaspac has invested heavily in environmental protection, natural gas implementation, and electrostatic precipitators to reduce SOX, NOX, and CO₂ emissions.

Our Services

End-to-end glass packaging services

Applied Ceramic Label (ACL)

Permanent ceramic labelling directly on glass for superior branding durability — resistant to abrasion, water, and UV.

Colouring Forehearth Technology

Produce glass containers in a wide range of colours directly from the furnace — offering great marketing advantages from a branding perspective.

In-House Design Studio

Complete service for bespoke glass packaging — from initial concept to manufacture and decoration for niche products and big brands.

Coating Services

Cold end and hot end coatings for improved scratch resistance, surface protection, and enhanced aesthetics for premium packaging.

Mould Shop

In-house mould manufacturing capability ensuring rapid turnaround for new bottle shapes — moulds ready in under 2 months for NPD launches.

Packaging Options

Comprehensive packaging solutions including bulk pallet packaging, retail-ready options, and custom pack configurations for every need.

Our History

Five decades of glass innovation

1972

AGI Founded — 80 Tonnes/day capacity

1981

HSIL acquires AGI, upgrades to 180 TPD

1996

ISO 9000 Certification achieved

2000

1st New Furnace (F-2) → 450 TPD capacity

2004

2nd Furnace (F-3) → 600 TPD capacity

2009

Bhongir Greenfield plant → 1,100 TPD

2011

First exports begin

2012

Largest single-location glass plant in Asia — 1,600 TPD

2016

Electrostatic Precipitators for SOX, NOX, CO₂ reduction

2017

TPM, LEAN & 6 SIGMA introduced

2019

Natural Gas implementation to curb carbon emissions
